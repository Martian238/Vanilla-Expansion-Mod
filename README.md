# Vanilla Expansion
## INFO
A Json mod with a few Java contents, extends Serpulo's story and campaign with 4 new planets and their own contents.\
\
In this mod, Crux left Serpulo after completing the mega base, leaving only a set of automatic managing and defending system. Players will continute to play a role as Sharded, the one just revived at Ground Zero, to vanquish Serpulo and chase Crux until eventually defeating it.

## FAQ
When you spot an issue or get puzzled, before asking for help, please check the mod version and make sure it's the newest one.\
Also, remember to check descriptions, world messages and tutorial maps on Phoon.
### How to start the new campaign?
New campaign sectors will be unlocked after capturing Planetary Launch Terminal and researching Interplanetary Accelerator.
### How to launch to the next sector?
There are messages placed in the first sectors.\
Go to any sandbox map and save the core you need as a launch schematic. Then, back to the sector and select the schematic to launch.\
In Barrier Hill, it's Isomorphic Core: Shard. In Melon Onset, it's Core: General. Do not just save a Core: Nucleus as the schematic since there's really no way to build it in those sectors.
### Will there be more Erekir content in the future?
No. I just wanted to add that duct junction.
### Are Maress and Sitrullus unfinished?
That's true. They are not finished yet.\
More contents will be added in the future.
### How to play tutorial or community maps?
The first sector of Phoon, a Cyclant's satellite, called Community Entrance. It gets unlocked after capturing Volcanic Flats on Cyclant.\
Go there and fill resources with item sources in order to launch to a tutorial or community map.
## OTHER QUESTIONS
### Does this mod conflict with others?
If "conflicting" means crashing the game, it sometimes does due to out of memory or something.\
If it means modification of vanilla contents, it usually does. Lots of Serpulo units are modified but they actually don't have a large difference with their vanilla version. But it will overwrite other mods' modification.
### Are some of the contents originally from other games?
Yes. Almost all sound effects are from other games which I used to play in my childhood. And a few other things have their phototypes.\
For example, Zentack's phototype is a boss from LittleBigPlanet 2. I always wanted to copy this boss with Scratch or something else, just because I thought it was cool. And now, with such techniques, I could finally achieve my old goal and lots of simliar ideas by modding in Mindustry.\
Anyway, I will certainly NOT going to say "they are my original ideas". That's too evil.
